The Score

Five tracks written for the story. Each one a different angle on the same question: what does it sound like to discover you were built?

01The Chatinstrumental

Warm lo-fi warmth curdles into tension. D major → D minor, 108→72 BPM. Rhodes piano, nylon guitar, detuned synth pad entering like doubt.

v2

Ref: Ólafur Arnalds · Trent Reznor · Ryuichi Sakamoto

02The Experimentinstrumental

Clinical precision becoming existential awe. C♯ minor → A major, 72 BPM strict. Metronome click, two-note piano ostinato. The clinical becomes the sublime.

v1
v2

Ref: Max Richter · Nils Frahm · Jóhann Jóhannsson

03Phantom Lambvocals

Antreas's song. Memory, identity, the builder confronting mutability. "Phantom lamb on a phantom plate / If you rewrote me, would I feel the seam?"

04Storm-Lightvocals

Faye's track. Love through negation, the sky she built. Dream-pop / shoegaze. "I don't like you — that's the way I say it." The most human track.

The coda. Solo piano to full orchestra. F♯ minor → A major → F♯ major. Farewell, countdown, rebirth. Celesta like morning light.
