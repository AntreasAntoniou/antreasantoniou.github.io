Axiotic AI research programme

DAEDALUS

A programme for resource-rational AI architectures: spend computation where information gain is highest, rather than processing everything uniformly.

The brain runs general intelligence on roughly 20 watts—about three bananas a day. Five scientific pillars test a single proposition: more capable systems may come from better organised computation, not only from more of it.

Research architecture

Five core scientific pillars

Each pillar is a research direction. The programme is designed to make its assumptions inspectable and its claims testable. All numerical programme objectives are research targets, not achieved results.

Attention allocation

SOMA

Allocate deep attention where uncertainty is highest.

Research target: equivalent quality at no more than 50% of the FLOPs.

Relational reasoning

ReSA

Replace fixed dot-product similarity with learned relational scoring.

Research target: ≥5% compositional gain at compute parity.

Memory and recursion

MNEME

Persistent differentiable read/write memory with recursive latent refinement.

Research target: ≥50% less context degradation over 10× context.

World modelling

Intuitus

Multimodal world modelling and cheap internal simulation.

Research target: ≥2× transfer efficiency.

Temporal structure

CHRONOS

Learn temporal structure from past, present, and future prediction.

Research target: exceed frontier performance per FLOP.

Working record

Evidence, including the checks that did not go our way

This is a constrained record of cleared observations and infrastructure, not a claim that the programme has reached its intended destination. Provenance: corrected internal DAEDALUS audit. The linked public TSP repository is an earlier snapshot; its run count and phase-transition terminology are superseded.

Controlled experimental base

432 controlled runs, 144 conditions, 3 seeds.

Sharp capability transitions

Observed 0→100% capability transitions within Δp=0.05. These sharp capability transitions are not presented as proven phase transitions.

CHRONOS check

At 150M, the pure triad objective lost to next-token prediction on validation loss. A crossover in another scale or data regime remains a falsifiable hypothesis.

Research infrastructure
